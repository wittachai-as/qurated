QURATED

by Dept. of International Trade Promotion, presents the latest fashion collections from the incubation program. The 61 new collections from Thai fashion designers/entrepreneurs including: womenswear, menswear, childrenswear, leather goods and accessories, aim at presenting more than 600 new merchandises for Spring/Summer 2021 to the domestic and international buyers.

The incubation series are curated by a panel of leading Thai fashion designers including:

  • Asst.Prof.Dr.Anothai CholachatpinyoQurator
  • Araya IndraTheatre
  • Bandit RassameerojGreyhound
  • Chaiyose PechdasadaTango
  • Jitsing SomboonGreyhound
  • Nat MangkangKloset
  • Ornvimol Seniwongse Na AyudhyaGreyhound
  • Saksit PisalasupongsTube Gallery
  • Sirichai DaharanontTheatre
  • Siriorn TeankaprasithPainkiller

The Qurated Fashion Incubation Project provides a creative framework and intense workshop series such as; fashion brand building, market positioning, collection development, commercial presentation and digital marketing. The design direction based on the Qurated trend forecast for Spring/Summer 2021 sub-visions into 4 major trends: New Local, Soul-Made, Urban Symphony, and Disruptive Identity. Each trend derived from lifestyles and consumer movements to forecast future market segments.
