ABOUT
QURATED FASHION
INCUBATION PROJECT 2022
Qurated X ICONCRAFT is a well-known co-creation/incubation project, in cooperation with DITP (Dept. of International Trade Promotion) Thailand, and ICONCRAFT, the modern multi-brand Thai craft store at ICONSIAM and Siam Discovery in Bangkok, Thailand. This Project brings together Thai fashion apprentices under the mentorship of experienced design gurus to find opportunities to provide market exposure as well as to commercialize pieces of designed items, debuting them for exhibitions.

The project is dedicated to unique fashion creations with wisdom found solely in Thai, contemporary craftmanship, and inspiring creativity. The Qurated X ICONCRAFT 2022 Project has incubated 40 talented brands/entrepreneurs, developed fashion collections for Spring/Summer 2023. Design apprentices selected from all over Thailand have participated the intense series of seminars and workshops including fashion trend forecast, market demand analysis, brand building, design process, collection making, digital marketing etc. Famous fashion experts and gurus joined as the advisory board have guided and mentored each apprentice from the start till the end.
