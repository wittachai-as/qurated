QURATED TREND FORUM

The trend forum is specially created for Qurated Fashion Incubation Project in order to present the originality of Thai designers. The forecast for Spring/Summer 2020 is divided into 3 major trends. Each trend forecasts theme, fashion direction, consumers’ mood, behaviors, color palette, and key visuals, in the way in which designers/brands in the project can be used as an inspiration and design direction.

LUCID ENVISION SS2020

Subjective Fulfillments, Pro-active,

The passing decade has been about Information, Awareness, Consciousness, and Sustainability. The attempts to reconnect the senses between individualistic values, lifestyles, and consumer’s culture; are just the beginning of the next chapter. The feeling of being lost subside, replace with aspirations and personal envision of the future.

Lucid Envision suggests multiple intuitive approaches to the concept of (self-actualization) living, growing, and listen to personal style evocations. Through the extension of the purpose and important of design, to support the recurring needs of each individualistic group. The process of global-actualization is beginning, each individuals are in the process of understanding personal potentials for global-impact. Wealth and prosperity that is explored through wellness, and improvement of overall life’s quality.

And for the rising individualistic realist generation, approaching the world as a cross culture learning ground, along with global-mindset and self-confidence, they are ready and willing to contribute to the progression of the society. But to some, who aren’t afraid to defy the societal norms, and challenge the aesthetic standards. From mass exhaustion, and boredom, which led to personal reconstructive time, the confusing time is coming to an end, the mass is looking forward to new decade with hopeful intentions. Fashion will be celebrated again, but this time from authentic, personal perspective, so the search for a new positioning is in progress.
