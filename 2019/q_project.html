Qurated Fashion Incubation Project (Q) by DITP or Dept. of International Trade Promotion, presents the latest fashion collections from the incubation program. The 58 new collections from Thai fashion designers/entrepreneurs including: textiles, womenswear, menswear, childrenswear, leather goods and accessories, aim at supplying more than 700 new merchandises for Spring/Summer 2020 to the domestic and international buyers at STYLE BANGKOK 2019 (OCT). The incubation series are curated by a panel of Leading Thai fashion designers, marketing and patterners including:

The Qurated Fashion Incubation Project provides an established framework and intense workshop series such as; fashion brand building, market positioning, collection development and commercial presentation. The design direction based on the Qurated trend forecast for Spring/Summer 2020 sub-visions into 3 major trends: Natural Standard, Maverick Youth, Subjective Matters. Each trend derived from lifestyles and consumer movements to forecast future market segments.
