Global Mindset,
Extending Borders,
Worldly Personal View

In the cross-culture world, experiences are abundant and easy to reach. What’s needed for young adults is the authenticity, the values of experience which will come mainly from personal immersion, and first-hand experience. For the culture and education are the key to self-actualization. Coming out of the shell is a natural process, into extending individual’s boarders, extending the boundaries, and set global mindset values. For the application of the trend, clothes for traveling and outdoor sports are prime focus. Design inspired from cyber culture, Orientalism and exoticism. Uniqueness comes from a mixture of tailoring shape and intense color scheme with a touch of craft techniques, indigenous print and weave designs.
