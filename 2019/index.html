Q FASHION MARK

Qurated Fashion Incubation Project (Q) by DITP or Dept. of International Trade Promotion, presents the latest fashion collections from the incubation program. The 58 new collections from Thai fashion designers/entrepreneurs including: textiles, womenswear, menswear, childrenswear, leather goods and accessories, aim at supplying more than 700 new merchandises for Spring/Summer 2020 to the domestic and international buyers at STYLE BANGKOK 2019 (OCT).
